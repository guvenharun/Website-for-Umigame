Anomalistic Revolution

As a software developer, you've created a software that provides correct answers to all questions. Now, you want to test this invention by solving the city's biggest mystery. But seeking the absolute truth in the enigmatic realm of zeros and ones comes with a price. Your choices may steer you toward either inner peace or inner chaos.

About Us

Founded in 2024 at an incubation center in Türkiye, our studio, Umigame, specializes in developing story-oriented games. Our team consists of five dedicated members.
